Our Heritage

Five Decades of
Golden Tradition

Founded in Kottayam, Kerala, Thoppil Jewellery has been a cornerstone of authentic gold craftsmanship since 1972. Every piece is a testament to the rich cultural traditions of Kerala.

We blend age-old techniques with contemporary sensibilities — creating jewellery that feels both timeless and freshly alive.
